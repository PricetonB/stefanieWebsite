In this deeply provocative--evocative?--study, author Stefanie Hutcheson takes the reader on a 28-day journey. Deliberately not using names nor specific events, Hutcheson invites readers to fill in those blanks, those scenarios with their own situations as a marriage is explored.

Lets Do This Write!

When you see the woman who used to be your next-door neighbor in an online writing group meeting, what do you do? She and her family packed up in the middle of the night two years ago. And now--albeit she's using a disguise--there she is, right back in your life, and you wonder. You wonder. You use your computer skills to track her down. And then what? What happens when you find out the reason she left and do you let her know the reason she needs to come back home?
